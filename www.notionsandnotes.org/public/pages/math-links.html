Mathematics Links

Here are some links filtered out from my bookmarks. Only those open to the public domain are given. For any suggestions or comments regarding this page, please contact me via Twitter.

Papers

  • ArXiv for math papers. Use ArXiv mailing lists for various ArXiv categories.
  • Numdam for papers from French journals. EGA, SGA, Publ. IHES., etc..
  • GDZ, the Göttinger Digitalisierungszentrum for old German papers, Inv. Math., etc.

Subject interests

  • Number Theory Web for number theorists.
  • EAGER-GEN, A European mailing list for Algebraic Geometry.
  • The n-Category café and nLab.
  • Commutative Algebra.
  • Foliations.
  • LFMDB, L-functions, modular forms database.

General

  • History of Mathematics, the MacTutor site.
  • Mathematical Genealogy.
  • Outofprintmath : Out of print mathematics books.
  • Proof wiki.
  • Mathematics at Stackexchange is a place for math queries. Mathoverflow is somewhat similar.

Institutions, News and Magazines

  • Clay Mathematical Institute
  • Fondation SM Paris
  • American Mathematical Society.
  • Notices of the AMS.
  • Quanta

About

Mainly notes, activity log, and sometimes personal blog, of an amateur mathematician, by which it is meant that the author did go through graduate school, but did not complete it to a Ph. D.. Obviously he still maintains interest in the same. Resuming mathematics full-time seems hard against the severe demands of life. The years rush by, but the dream persists. Borrowing Robert Browning,

Ah, but a man's reach should exceed his grasp,
Or what's a heaven for?
